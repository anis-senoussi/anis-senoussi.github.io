I am a postdoctoral researcher @Gulliver lab. in ESPCI (PSL University, Paris) in the group of Yannick Rondelez & Guillaume Gines. I am interested in the self-organization of biological molecules and living systems.
I was previously @Jean Perrin lab. in the group of André Estévez-Torres & Jean-Christophe Galas (Morphogenesis in molecular systems group, Sorbonne Université, Paris) where I did my PhD.
